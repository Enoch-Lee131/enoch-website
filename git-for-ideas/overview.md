# Git-for-Ideas

## What is it?

**Git-for-Ideas** is an AI-powered document evolution tracking system that helps users understand how their writing changes over time and enhance their writing using AI agents.

Think of it as **Git for your thoughts** - version control meets AI-assisted writing.

## Problem Statement

Writers, researchers, and knowledge workers struggle to:
- Track how their ideas evolve over time
- Understand what changes were made and why
- Get intelligent feedback on their writing
- Maintain multiple branches of thought experiments

## Solution

Git-for-Ideas provides:
- **Version Control**: Like Git, but optimized for prose and ideas
- **AI Analysis**: Understand the semantic changes, not just text diffs
- **Smart Suggestions**: AI agents that help improve your writing
- **Trajectory Tracking**: See how your ideas evolved from first draft to final version

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React, TailwindCSS
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (via Supabase)
- **AI**: OpenAI GPT-4 for analysis and suggestions
- **Deployment**: Vercel (frontend), Railway (backend)

## Key Features

### 1. Document Versioning
Every change to your document is tracked with semantic understanding, not just character-level diffs.

### 2. Branch Management
Create multiple branches to explore different directions for your writing, just like code branches.

### 3. AI-Powered Diffs
See not just what changed, but understand the semantic difference between versions.

### 4. Writing Trajectories
Visualize how your document evolved from initial brainstorm to final draft.

### 5. Collaborative Editing
Multiple users can work on the same document with proper version control.
